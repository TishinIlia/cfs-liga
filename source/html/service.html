Fire Extinguisher Hydrostatic Testing or Replacement

Unused and maintained fire extinguishers should last a very long time, but let’s face it—nothing’s perfect.

If your fire extinguisher is more than 12 years old, the NFPA and local authorities require you to have it hydro-tested or replaced. A fire extinguisher hydro-test will make sure the shell of your fire extinguisher has no cracks or leaks that can cause it to malfunction when you need it most. If it turns out we need to replace your fire extinguishers, that’s no problem!
