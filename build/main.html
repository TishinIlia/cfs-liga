Kitchen Fire Suppression System

ANSUL restaurant fire suppression system

The kitchen Fire Suppression system is designed to protect areas associated with auxiliary grease extraction equipment and cooking equipment such as various types of fryers, griddles, range tops, broilers, char-broilers and woks. The system is ideally suited for commercial kitchens in fast food chains, retail food courts, diners, convenience stores, catering facilities, mobile food trucks, hotels and casinos, stadiums, schools, hospitals and more.
